Canvas? Canvas?

Explanation

If you've ever played with a graphing calculator, you'll know that the functions f(x)=|x| and f(x)=-|x| make a hallway-looking thing (put in f(x)=xsin(x) for some funky wallpaper). It's a simple one-point perspective centered at the origin. So all I had to do was create a single point that I then used to draw a couple polygons from the edges of the screen. To make it repeat and move in a circular fashion, I took the tangent of the vanishing point's x position.

Beyond that, the only other trick was to add in a gradient background to really sell the idea of perspective. It makes it look like the hallway darkens the further down you look.
